About Salem

Fun Fact: Salem hosts the Oregon State Fair, one of the oldest state fairs in the country. The fair showcases agriculture, creative arts, live entertainment, and exciting amusement rides.

Salem, Oregon is a large city with a population of over 170,000 residents. It was incorporated in 1857 and is located in the Willamette Valley region of the state. Salem is classified as an urban city and serves as the capital of Oregon. In terms of average income level, Salem is generally on par with the rest of the state.
