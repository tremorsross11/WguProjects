About Portland

Fun Fact: A coin toss decided the name for Portland. Had the other side of the coin landed face up, the city would have been named Boston.

Portland, Oregon is a vibrant city located in the Pacific Northwest region of the United States. With a population of over 650,000, it is the largest city in Oregon and the second most populous city in the Pacific Northwest. Portland was officially incorporated in 1845, making it one of the oldest cities on the West Coast. Situated in the northern part of the state, Portland is classified as an urban city and serves as the economic and cultural hub of the region. In terms of average income, Portland tends to have a higher average income level compared to the rest of the state, reflecting its diverse economy and thriving job market.
